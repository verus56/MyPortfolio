It allows hospitals, clinics, and medical professionals to manage patient data, appointments, medical reports, and other critical information in a user-friendly environment. ## üìç Abstract

The Healthcare Management System offers a comprehensive solution to manage patient records, appointments, medical reports, and more. Designed with a focus on security, accessibility, and ease of use, this system aims to automate and simplify various administrative and medical processes. From patient registration to doctor scheduling and report generation, everything is handled efficiently through an intuitive web interface.

## üåü Features

- **Patient Management**: Store and manage detailed patient records with easy search and filtering options.
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Schedule appointments with doctors, including room assignments and time management.
- **Medical Report Generation**: Generate, store, and retrieve medical reports with patient details.
- **Admin Dashboard**: A centralized dashboard for administrators to manage users, doctors, and appointments.
- **User Authentication**: Secure login and registration for different user roles (admin, doctor, secretary, etc.).
- **Search & Filter**: Quickly search for patients, appointments, or medical records with powerful filtering options.
- **Notifications**: Receive alerts for upcoming appointments and updates on patient status.
- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for desktop and mobile use, ensuring easy access on any device.

## ü§ñ AI Health Agent

- **AI-Powered Diagnosis Assistant**: Helps medical professionals with preliminary diagnosis based on symptoms and patient history.
- **Health Monitoring**: AI agent that tracks patient vital signs and alerts medical staff of concerning changes.
- **Smart Appointment Recommendations**: Uses machine learning to suggest optimal appointment times based on patient history and doctor availability.
- **Automated Follow-ups**: AI system that sends personalized follow-up instructions and reminders to patients.
